EdIsDim

Converts stereo tracks to mid/side, applies processing, and then converts back to stereo.

Key Controls and Settings:

* Converts stereo track into mid/side on left and right channels
	+ Range: N/A
	+ Type: Function
* Balances mid/side content for gain staging
	+ Range: N/A
	+ Type: Control
* Reverses gain staging applied in conversion process
	+ Range: N/A
	+ Type: Button

Caveats:
None specified.

Best Use Cases:

* Wide stereo reverb with a fresh take.
* Saturation of center and lively width using density plugins.
* Routing techniques in DAW: send L and R to separate busses, merge back to stereo bus.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
Balance mid/side content for gain staging.
Use EdIsDim as an intentional part of synth sound design.
