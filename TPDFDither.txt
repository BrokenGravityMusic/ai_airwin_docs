TPFD Dither


A plugin that dithers audio signals to prevent distortion when converting high-resolution formats into lower-bit-depth ones.


Key Controls and Settings:


* Mix: A blend control for mixing the processed sound with the original
	+ Range: 0% to 100%

Caveats:


Use this plugin without adjusting its settings or opening it in the DAW. It is a simple, high-resolution implementation of the TPDF algorithm that works well in transitioning sounds into silence.


Best Use Cases:


This plugin is best used for converting high-resolution audio signals into lower-bit-depth formats without introducing distortion.
