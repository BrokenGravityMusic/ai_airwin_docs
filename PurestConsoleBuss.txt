Best Use Cases:
Ideal for use as a clean, uncolored console bus in mixing and mastering applications. Suitable for mastering stages, post-processing, or whenever a clean and uncolored signal path is required.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
For best results, set Input Level Control to 0 dB to prevent coloration, adjust Output Level Control to match the desired signal level. Adjust the Pre-Delay to match your material's timing needs. Experiment with different console characterizations to find the perfect fit for your sound.
