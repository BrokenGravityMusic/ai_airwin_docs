Verbity

Key Controls and Settings:

* `Darkness`: Lowpass filter control (range: 0-1.0).
* `Wetness`: Reverb level control (range: 0-1.0). Increases wet signal up to 0.5 without attenuating dry signal, then starts attenuating dry while keeping wet at full crank.

Best Use Cases:

* Custom reverb spaces for stems and busses in Console mix.
* Adding depth and atmosphere to centered audio content.
