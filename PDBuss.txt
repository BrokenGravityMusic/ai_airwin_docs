PurestDrive

Saturation plugin that adds subtle tone and character, ideal for adding warmth and texture without overpowering subtleties.

Key Controls and Settings:

* `Intensity`: A slider control from 0 to 1 that determines how much saturation is applied.
    + Range: 0% to 100%
* `Apply`: The combination of the dry and saturated signals, calculated dynamically based on frequency content.
    + Type: Blend control

Best Use Cases:
Ideal for adding subtle saturation to individual tracks or busses, preserving airy highs and subtleties while enhancing dense material. Suitable for a wide range of genres and musical styles.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
* Start with low intensity values (0.1-0.3) and adjust to taste.
* Use PurestDrive as a final effect before mastering or on individual tracks where additional character is desired.
* Experiment with different saturation algorithms and techniques to find the best fit for your music.
