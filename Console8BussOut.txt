Key Controls and Settings:

* `Topology:` Choose the desired topology for the plugin's output (channel, submix, or buss)
    + Type: Channel/Submix/Buss
* `Analog Simulation:` Select from various analog simulation modes (e.g. preamp, EQ, compressor) to add depth to your sound
    + Range: N/A
    + Type: Analog Simulation type
* Gain Staging: Adjust the gain level of the simulated signal
    + Range: N/A
    + Type: Gain Staging

Best Use Cases:

* Adding an analog "flavor" to plugin outputs for mixing and mastering
* Enhancing low-end clarity and warmth in bass-heavy mixes
* Creating a hybrid digital-analog sound for unique character
