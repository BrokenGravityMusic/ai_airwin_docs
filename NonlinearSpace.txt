A flexible reverb plugin with filter controls and acoustic space simulation.

Key Controls and Settings:

* `Filter Controls:` 
	+ Low-pass filter: controls low-end frequency response
	+ High-pass filter: controls high-end frequency response
* `Acoustic Space Simulation:`
	+ Space size: sets room size and ambiance
	+ Reverb time: sets decay time of reverb
* `Sample Rate:` sets buffer length based on sample rate (44.1K, 48K, 88.2K, or 96K)

Best Use Cases:

* Gated reverb sounds for drums or percussion
* Ambient pads or textures
* Large stadium or hall simulations
