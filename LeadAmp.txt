LeadAmp

An amp sim with a clear, penetrating, vocal tone.

Key Controls and Settings:

* `Highpass Filter:` Sets the high-frequency boost or cut to taste.
* Gain Stages:
    + Cascading gain stages with ultrasonic filtering between them.
    + Ultrasonic filtering can be tuned to suppress aliasing at low sample rates (96k recommended).
* Cab Simulator: Dynamically convolved, truncated tone color.

Best Use Cases:
Ideal for creating clear and penetrating lead tones, especially those with a vocal or Vox-like quality. Suitable for lead guitar and dirt guitar tones with a clear, vocal quality.
