Srsly

Psychoacoustic stereo processor, similar in effect to a Hughes SRS processor.

Key Controls and Settings:

* `Q:` Adjusts the effectiveness of the processor.
	+ Range: Not specified
	+ Type: Gain control

Caveats:
May not accurately replicate the sound of a real Hughes SRS processor. Effectiveness may vary depending on the user's specific audio material and preferences.

Best Use Cases:
Refining subtle stereo spatiality in audio mixes. Enhancing the sense of width or depth in individual tracks.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
Experiment with different Q settings to find the optimal balance for your particular audio content.
