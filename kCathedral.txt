kCathedral

A dedicated sound space reverb with its own unique algorithm, inspired by Bricasti Cathedral preset.

Key Controls and Settings:

* Dry/Wet control:
	+ Adjusts the blend of the original sound and the reverb.
	+ Use on an aux bus for full wet or inline with a small amount (e.g. 0.01 to 0.03) for subtlety.
* No additional controls mentioned.

Caveats: None specific to this plugin.

Best Use Cases:

* Realism: Hide the reverb behind other sounds by keeping it quiet.
* Spatiality: Use kCathedral to create a sense of depth and space in your mix.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
To get the best results from kCathedral, follow these tips:
Use the plugin on an aux bus for full wet signal or inline with a small amount (e.g. 0.01 to 0.03).
Adjust the Dry/Wet control to achieve the desired balance between dry and wet signals.
Experiment with different settings to find the optimal amount of reverb for your specific sound.
