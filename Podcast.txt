A pile of curve-style compressors in series for dynamic range control.

Key Controls and Settings:

* Multiple Compressors: A series of 5 curve-style compressors in a chain
    + Each compressor can be adjusted independently, but no additional settings are provided
* Dry/Wet Control: A blend between the original signal and the compressed signal
    + Range: 0% to 100%
    + Type: Blend control

Best Use Cases:
As a 'safety compressor' for aggressively smashed sounds, like drums
As a clean buss comp with low gain reduction
