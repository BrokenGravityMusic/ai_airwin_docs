Infinity

Key Controls and Settings:

* Filter: Sweep frequencies in and out, from deep space to bright and unnatural textures.
* Size control: Resize the space from gong-like tones to the depths of galaxies.
* Damping control: Apply a subtle darkening effect, useful for creating darker spaces.
* Dual Mono Effect: Adds sustain at distinct places in the stereo image.

Best Use Cases:

Creating ambient spaces that sustain and layer sounds forever.
Ideal for pads, sustained textures and as a standalone instrument or combined with other plugins.
