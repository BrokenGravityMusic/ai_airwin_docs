Precious

A VST plugin that simulates guitar amp and distortion effects.

Key Controls and Settings:

* Color Intensity: Adjusts the strength of the simulated color effect.
	+ Range: 0-100
* Distortion Amount: Controls the amount of distortion applied to the signal.
	+ Range: 0-100

Best Use Cases:
Ideal for adding color or simulation to audio signals, or for distorting audio in music production.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
Experiment with different Color Intensity and Distortion Amount settings to achieve the desired effect.
