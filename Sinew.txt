Slew Clipper

Morphs and transforms percussive sounds, adding a unique "grind" to instruments or gluing stuff together into a retro sound.

Key Controls and Settings:

* `Threshold:` Adjusts the level at which slew clipping engages.
    + Range: 
    + Type: control
* `Slew:` Sets the rate at which the clipper darkens treble in an unusual way. Can be adjusted for extreme values.
    + Range: 
    + Type: control

Best Use Cases:

* Morphing percussive sounds
* Adding a unique "grind" to instruments
* Gluing stuff together into a retro sound
* Using on reverb sends to amplify distant loud sound in a room or space

Tips on Optimal Settings:
Experiment with Threshold and Slew settings to achieve the desired effect. Pay attention to how the plugin engages, as it may not always be audible when adjusting subtle values.
