Tube2

A sophisticated tube simulator with input trim and advanced processing, designed for finishing up audio with a unique "tube" sound.

Key Controls and Settings:

* `Input Trim:` 
	+ Range: 0.0 - 1.0
	+ Default: 0.5
* `TUBE Control:` Adjusts the amount of distortion and saturation.
	+ Range: 
	+ Type: 
* Mix: A blend control for mixing the processed sound with the original

Caveats:
Not designed for running Tube2 into Tube2, as it may not produce optimal results.

Best Use Cases:

* Finishing up audio with a unique "tube" sound
* Adding distortion and saturation to audio

Tips on Optimal Settings:
* Adjust the input trim to 0.5 for a level-matched signal.
* Use the TUBE control to add desired amounts of saturation and distortion.
* Back off the TUBE control for more linear tone and reduced distortion.
