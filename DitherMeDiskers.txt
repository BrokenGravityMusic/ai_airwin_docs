DitherMeDiskers

Generates a type of noise, known as "dither", used to reduce quantization errors in digital audio.

Key Controls and Settings:

* `Bit Depth:` Sets the resolution of the dither signal (set to 16-bit by default).
	+ Range: N/A
	+ Type: Control
* `Noise Shape:` Adjusts the spectral shape of the dither signal.
	+ Range: N/A
	+ Type: Control

Caveats:
None

Best Use Cases:

* Reducing quantization errors in digital audio recordings
* Improving the dynamic range and overall quality of digital audio signals

Tips on Optimal Settings:
Adjust the bit depth to match the resolution of your digital audio signal.
