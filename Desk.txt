Desk

A subtle analog modeling plugin that glues and thickens the sound, similar to a true analog console.

Key Controls and Settings:

* Headroom: Adjusts the amount of headroom available before clipping.
    + Range: Up to 30 dB
* Tone Shaping: Controls the amount of tone shaping applied to the signal.
    + Range: -20 dB to +10 dB

Best Use Cases:

* Adding a subtle glue and thickness to individual channels or submixes.
* Enhancing the overall sound of the 2-buss or master bus.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
To get the best results from Desk, use it in moderation. Applying too much processing can degrade the sound quality. Start with subtle adjustments and gradually increase the effect as needed to achieve your desired tone.
