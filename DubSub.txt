DubSub

A fully featured bass doctor plugin.

`Key Controls and Settings:`
* `Treble Grind`: Bass guitar presence circuit with Inverse/Out control for subtracting or adding distortion.
	+ Controls: Inverse, Out (0-100), Dry/Wet ratio.
* `Crossover`: Determines signal split between Treble Grind and bass sections.
	+ Tip: Set low to make it track bass better.
* `Bass Drive`: Adjustable Head Bump control for main bass section.
	+ Control: Level (0-100).
* `Bass Voicing`: Controls depth of bass boost.
	+ Control: Level (0-100).
* `Sub Drive`: Adjustable drive control for octave-divided bass section.
	+ Control: Level (0-100).
* `Sub Voicing`: Controls depth of sub-bass boost.
	+ Control: Level (0-100).
* `Sub Inv/Out`: Attenuverter for subtracting or adding sub-bass signal.

`Best Use Cases:`
Ideal for bass-heavy music productions where a strong low-end presence is desired. Can also be used creatively to enhance or alter sound.

`Tips on Optimal Settings:`
Set the crossover low to improve bass tracking. Adjust the Treble Grind settings to add or subtract presence to the signal. Set the Sub Drive and Voicing controls deep but mix in only a small amount of the effect to create a cleaner deep bass sound.
