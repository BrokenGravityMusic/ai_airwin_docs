Bright EQ Simulator

Simulates a bright EQ effect, requiring 44.1K sample rate.

Key Controls and Settings:

* Sample Rate: Set to 44.1K for optimal performance.
    + Range: Not applicable (fixed)
    + Type: Integer
* No additional controls or settings are specified.

Caveats:
Effects may shift up in frequency at higher sample rates, potentially making them inaudible. Not compatible with sample rates other than 44.1K.

Best Use Cases:
Use in contexts where a bright EQ effect is desired and 44.1K sample rate is available.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
Set sample rate to 44.1K for best results.
