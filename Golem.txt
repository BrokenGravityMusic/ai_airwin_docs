Golem

A stereo track blender that allows balancing of input levels and applying delay to one side.

Key Controls and Settings:

* Input Balancer: Adjusts the relative levels of the left and right channels.
* Delay Control: Applies a delay to either the left or right channel, allowing for fine-tuned phase relationships between mics. Delay can be inverted or increased in range.
	+ Range: Option to increase the range of available delays.
* Mix: A blend control for mixing the processed sound with the original
	+ Range: 0% to 100%

Best Use Cases:

Blending two mics on a guitar cab for metalhead-style sound, creating subtle phase relationships between microphones.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
Make small adjustments to the Input Balancer for subtle balance changes. Experiment with delay settings and inversion options to achieve desired cancellations and tone shaping.
