BuildATPDF

A dither-making toolkit that allows you to customize the sound of the noise.

Key Controls and Settings:

* Middle sliders:
    + Set to -1 and +1 for DoublePaul-style high-pitched tone
    + Adjust other sliders to tailor the sound of the noise
* Highpass control:
    + Gentle or obvious tonal shaping
    + Can be darkened while preserving dither functionality

Best Use Cases:
Tailoring custom dither noises for specific audio applications, mixing and mastering.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
Adjusting surrounding sliders to create varying degrees of oscillation and tone, experimenting with different slider settings and tap combinations can produce unique effects.
