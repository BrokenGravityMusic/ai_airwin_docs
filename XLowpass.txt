XLowpass

Key Controls and Settings:

* `Distortion`: A control for adjusting the amount of internal distortion in the plugin.
    + Adjusting this control will affect the overall tone and character of the EQ.
* `Filter Type`: A selection menu for choosing between different biquad filter types (e.g. lowpass).
    + This setting determines the type of filtering applied to the audio signal.
* `Stage Count`: A control for adjusting the number of stages in the internal distortion circuit.
    + Increasing this value will add more complexity and character to the distortion.
* `Cutoff Frequency`: A control for adjusting the cutoff frequency of the lowpass filter.
    + Adjusting this value will affect the tone and resonance of the filtered signal.

Best Use Cases:

* Mixing and mastering: Use XLowpass as a creative tool for adding unique coloration and texture to your audio.
* Sound design: Experiment with XLowpass to create interesting and unusual sounds.
* Adding grit and character to audio signals.

Tips on Optimal Settings:

* Start by adjusting the Distortion control to taste, then experiment with different Filter Types and Stage Counts to find the desired sound.
* Pay attention to the Cutoff Frequency setting when using the lowpass filter, as it can greatly affect the tone of the filtered signal.
* Use XLowpass in conjunction with other plugins for more complex tones.
