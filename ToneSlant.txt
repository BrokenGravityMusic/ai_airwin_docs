ToneSlant

A super-transparent 'tilt EQ' with very low Q that produces an extremely transparent tone with a controllable corner point.

Key Controls and Settings:

* `Taps`: Sets the number of samples in the average block (1-100).
    + Range: 1-100
    + Type: Control
* `Highs`: Adjusts the frequency response of the tilt EQ.
    + Setting to +1 brings in the brightest highs.
    + Setting to -1 nulls out high frequencies.

Best Use Cases:

* Treble rolloff above a set point with fixed dB/oct response.
* Natural high-shelf controllable EQ.
* Total cancellation of high frequencies.
