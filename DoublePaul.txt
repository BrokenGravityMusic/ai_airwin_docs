DoublePaul

A highpassed TPDF dither with redistributed high-frequency noise energy.

Key Controls and Settings:

* None (default settings only)

Best Use Cases:
Ideal for mastering engineers seeking a quieter, more traditional TPDF dither sound with additional high-end detail. Suitable as an output dither for 16/24-bit formats.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
Compare DoublePaul to PaulDither and adjust your workflow accordingly based on personal preference.
