DoublePaul

A highpassed TPDF dither with redistributed high-frequency noise energy, ideal for mastering engineers seeking a quieter, more traditional sound with additional high-end detail.

Key Controls and Settings:

* None (default settings only)

Caveats:
None noted.

Best Use Cases:
Ideal for mastering engineers seeking a quieter, more traditional TPDF dither sound with additional high-end detail. Suitable as an output dither for 16/24-bit formats.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
No specific settings are required, as the plugin operates with default values. To achieve the best results, compare DoublePaul to PaulDither and adjust your workflow accordingly based on personal preference.
