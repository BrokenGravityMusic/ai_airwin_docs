RightoMono

Copies one channel to both channels losslessly.

Key Controls and Settings:

* None

Caveats:
Does not modify or alter the input signal in any way
Ideal for situations where a stereo input is available but only one side contains useful audio

Best Use Cases:
Working with camera or camcorder audio where the unused channel contains noise or interference
Converting a stereo input to mono without affecting the quality of the desired signal

Tips on Optimal Settings:
No settings are required; simply instantiate and use the plugin
