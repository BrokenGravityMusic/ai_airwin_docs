Mackity

An emulation of the input stage of a vintage Mackie 1202 mixer, providing a spongy slam and gleaming brain-fry overload.

Key Controls and Settings:

* Drive: Controls the intensity of the Mackie-style overdrive. Can be adjusted to achieve a range of tonal colors.
	+ Range: 0.1 to 10
* Insert Point: Simulates the effect of plugging into the insert points on the back of the original Mackie 1202, allowing for mid-signal manipulation.

Best Use Cases:

* Underground electronic music production
* DnB and other high-energy genres

Tips on Optimal Settings:

* Experiment with different Drive levels to find the perfect balance of slam and tone.
* Adjusting Insert Point can help shape the midrange frequency response.
