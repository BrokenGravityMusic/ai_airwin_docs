Mackity

An emulation of the input stage of a vintage Mackie 1202 mixer, providing a spongy slam and gleaming brain-fry overload.

Key Controls and Settings:
* Drive: Controls the intensity of the Mackie-style overdrive. Can be adjusted to achieve a range of tonal colors (0.1 to 10)
	+ Range: 0.1 to 10
	+ Type: Gain control
* Insert Point: Simulates the effect of plugging into the insert points on the back of the original Mackie 1202, allowing for mid-signal manipulation (0 to 100%)
	+ Range: 0 to 100%
	+ Type: Blend control

Caveats:
None specific to the plugin itself.

Best Use Cases:
* Underground electronic music production
* DnB and other high-energy genres
* Adding grit and texture to electronic or synthesized sounds

Tips on Optimal Settings:
Experiment with different Drive levels to find the perfect balance of slam and tone.
Adjusting Insert Point can help shape the midrange frequency response.
