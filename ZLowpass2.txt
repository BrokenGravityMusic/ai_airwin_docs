ZLowpass2

A low-pass filter that emulates the Emu e6400 Ultra lowpass in motion, with coefficient interpolation.

Key Controls and Settings:

* `Cutoff Frequency`: Adjusts the point at which the filter starts to attenuate high frequencies.
	+ Range: Not specified
	+ Type: Control
* `Resonance`: Controls the peak amplification of the frequency at the cutoff point.
	+ Range: Not specified
	+ Type: Control
* `Gain`: Provides a significant amount of gain for internal clipping in samplers.
	+ Range: Not specified
	+ Type: Control
* `Mix:` A blend control for mixing the processed sound with the original
	+ Range: Not specified

Best Use Cases:
Ideal for use as a sampler filter, providing a seamless blend of options and high gain for internal clipping. Suitable for DnB and neuro-sparkle effects.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
Sweeping the controls in motion to "play" the sampler EQ can help achieve the best results. Automating the controls can also enhance its performance.
