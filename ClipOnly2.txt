ClipOnly2

Suppresses the brightness of digital clipping without affecting unclipped samples, effective at high sample rates.

Key Controls and Settings:

CPU-efficient due to its alternate clipping technique.

Best Use Cases:
Mastering-grade clipping for music and audio mastering applications.
Safety clipping for heavy load and severe clipping scenarios.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
No settings need to be adjusted, as ClipOnly2 is a known quantity with a predictable sound.
Simply bypass the plugin in normal use, allowing it to operate at its default setting.
