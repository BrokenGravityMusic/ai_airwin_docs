BitShiftGain

Applies gain or cut by exact increments of 6 dB, using a 'bit shift' method to maintain mathematical precision.

Key Controls and Settings:

* `Number of Bits to Shift`: Selects the number of bits to shift up or down.
	+ Range: Not specified
	+ Type: Integer
* `Direction`: Determines whether to apply a boost (shift up) or pad (shift down).
	+ Range: Boost/Pad
	+ Type: Dropdown
* Mix: A blend control for mixing the processed sound with the original, not applicable.
	+ Range: Not specified
	+ Type: Not applicable

Caveats:
None specified.

Best Use Cases:
Ideal for precise gain adjustments and bit-accurate processing. Can be used for dithering and gain trimming with minimal degradation.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
* Apply 6 dB increments for best results.
* Be aware of the overall range of BitShiftGain, which can be huge.
* Consider using BitShiftGain in conjunction with other plugins to achieve desired effects.
