SubsOnly

A mix check plugin that shows only the extreme lows, similar to SlewOnly.

Key Controls and Settings:

* Switch: Turn the plugin on or off.
* Placement: Insert the plugin at the end of your master buss to hear the effect of your 2-buss chain.

Best Use Cases:
Use at the end of your master buss to hear what your 2-buss chain did to the sound. Especially useful for sanity checking when trying to achieve "commercially loud" volumes.
