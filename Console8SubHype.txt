Console8SubHype

A brightness and high-mid softening plugin for channels, submixes, or busses.

Key Controls and Settings:

* No controls; replaces Console8SubIn.
    + Swappable with Console8ChannelHype and Console8BussHype for different stages of processing.

Best Use Cases:
* On individual channels for direct effect
* On submixes to change the tone of multiple tracks at once
* On buss to apply hype tonality to the entire mix

Tips on Optimal Settings:
* Experiment with different stages and positions in the chain to achieve desired results.
* Pay attention to textural values like solidness, ethereal spaceyness, physical versus abstract, hard versus soft.
