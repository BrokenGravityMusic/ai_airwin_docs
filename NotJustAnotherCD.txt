DeRez

An analog-style bit and sample rate crusher with continuous adjustments.

Key Controls and Settings:

* Frequency control: Adjusts the sample-rate crushing continuously
	+ To sweep the sample-rate crushing, slide this control down.
* Resolution control: Adjusts the bit crushing continuously
	+ To sweep the bit crushing, slide this control down.
* Algorithm: Does a simple samplerate crush with soft transitions, and a bit crush by chopping away bit-sized amounts and then truncating

Caveats:
None listed.

Best Use Cases:
Use in DAWs to create continuous sweeps of sample rate or bit crushing effects. Ideal for experimental music production, sound design, and audio processing.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
Adjust Frequency and Resolution controls to taste to achieve desired sonic characteristics. Automation can be used to program continuous sweeps.
