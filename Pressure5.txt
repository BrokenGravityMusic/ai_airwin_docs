Pressure5

A compressor with adjustable vari-mu and 'new york' peak-retaining behaviors, featuring an unusual 'mewiness' control for dynamic inversion and super-squishing of audio.

Key Controls and Settings:

* `Mewiness`: Manages the ratio engagement intensity.
	+ Range: N/A
	+ Type: Control
* `PawClaw`: Adjusts how 'mewiness' handles transients, allowing for extra squishing on 'Paw' or letting them through more on 'Claw'.
	+ Range: N/A
	+ Type: Control
* `Speed`: A wide speed range for dense and distorted sounds.
	+ Range: N/A
	+ Type: Control
* `Output Control`: Boosts into a built-in ClipOnly2 for chunky hard clipping drive.
	+ Range: N/A
	+ Type: Boost (with built-in ClipOnly2)
* `ClipOnly2`: Suppresses brightness of digital clipping without affecting unclipped samples.
	+ Range: N/A
	+ Type: Built-in effect

Best Use Cases:
Final buss comp/clip stage for mixing and mastering.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
Pad the output control to bypass ClipOnly2 when not needed. Adjust 'mewiness' and 'PawClaw' controls to tailor compression behavior.
