Beam

A wordlength reducer that seeks to heighten sonority by reinforcing certain waveform slope angles.

Key Controls and Settings:

* Focus control: Adjusts the frequency range emphasized by Beam, with lower settings encouraging focus on deeper sounds and higher settings highlighting treble.
	+ Range: 0% to 100%
	+ Type: controls emphasis on different frequencies
* DeRez control: Fine-tunes sound quality without affecting noise floor.

Caveats:

* Noise floor can become unpredictable and loud, unlike other dither plugins like Dark that gate into silence or seamlessly blend into noise.

Best Use Cases:

* Suitable for audio processing where a unique sonic maximizer effect is desired.
* Emphasizing energy and depth in audio.
* Creating a unique sonic character.

Tips on Optimal Settings:

* Experiment with different Focus settings to find the optimal balance between deeper and treble sounds.
* Adjust DeRez control to fine-tune sound quality without affecting noise floor.
