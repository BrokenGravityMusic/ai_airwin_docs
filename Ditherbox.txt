Ditherbox

A switchable selection of dithers in 16 and 24 bit, plus monitoring tools.

Key Controls and Settings:

* `Dither Type`: Choose from various Airwindows dither plugins
    + Range: Select the most suitable dither type for your needs
    + Type: Varies by selection
* `Bit Depth`: Switch between 16-bit and 24-bit output
    + Range: Select based on output requirements (e.g. CD-quality 16-bit)
    + Type: Binary (16/24 bit)
* Monitoring Tools:
    + SlewOnly: Calibrated playback for Slew-only content
    + SubsOnly: Calibrated playback for Subs-only content
    + Silhouette: Calibrated noise replacement to evaluate dynamic behavior
        - Range: Varies by selection
        - Type: Noise replacement

Best Use Cases:

* Evaluating dynamic behavior and compression peaks
* Assessing the quality of a mix and identifying potential issues
* Comparing audio content with different dithers and bit depths

Tips on Optimal Settings:

* Experiment with different dithers to find the best fit for your music
* Adjust Bit Depth according to your output requirements (e.g. CD-quality 16-bit)
* Use Monitoring Tools to evaluate playback levels and dynamics
