ToTape7


Emulates the sound of classic tape machines with a range of controls for precise tweaking and creative experimentation.


Key Controls and Settings:

* `Highs Cutoff:` Sets the high-frequency cutoff for the tape saturation effect.
	+ Range: [not specified]
	+ Type: Frequency cutoff
* `Dubly Decode (Frequency & Amount):` Helps smooth out compressed sounds and control the texture of your audio.
	+ Range: Not specified ( frequency & amount)
	+ Type: Compressor
* `Tape Drive (Boost/Pad):` Adjusts the gain of the tape drive, allowing for clean gain or loudening of the signal.
	+ Range: 0% to ?
	+ Type: Gain control
* `Flutter:` Emulates the "3D tape" effect, where tape bends laterally and stretches.
	+ Range: Not specified
	+ Type: Effect (can be bypassed)
* `Bias:` Sets the biasing of the tape machine, which can help control unpleasant tizzy highs.
	+ Range: Not specified
	+ Type: Bias adjustment
* `Head Bump:` Emulates the sound of tape machine head bumps.
	+ Range: Not specified
	+ Type: Bass effect (with built-in highpass filter)

Best Use Cases:

* Create realistic tape saturation effects
* Dial in the desired sound using precise tweaking and creative experimentation

Tips on Optimal Settings:

* Slight overbiasing is recommended for most cases to control tizzy highs
* The Head Bump frequency can be adjusted independently of the overall head bump effect, allowing for creative voicing
