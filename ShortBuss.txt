ShortBuss

A plugin that chases second harmonic to add or remove it, providing an asymmetry effect by finding imbalance in the waveform.

Key Controls and Settings:

* `Balance`: Adjusts the balance between even harmonics.
* `Reverse Balance`: Applies reverse ShortBuss, fighting even harmonics and trying to take them away.
	+ Range: [insert text]
	+ Type: [insert text]
* `Asymmetry`: Adjusts the level of asymmetry in the waveform.
* `Second Harmonic`: Controls the amount of second harmonic added or removed from the signal.
	+ Range: 0% to 100%
	+ Type: [insert text]

Best Use Cases:

* Specializing in adding warmth to bass sounds without affecting other frequencies.
* Cleaning up low end slightly by removing even harmonics.
* Creating a unique conditioning effect on heavy distortion or bass-heavy mixes.
