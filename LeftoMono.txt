LeftoMono

Duplicates one channel to both (mono-to-stereo conversion), with no signal degradation.

Key Controls and Settings:

* No adjustable parameters. The plugin simply duplicates the input audio to both channels.
* Mix: A blend control for mixing the processed sound with the original
 Range: 0% to 100%

Best Use Cases:
 Useful for converting mono recordings or signals to stereo, without affecting the original audio.
