Caveats:

* Locked to 44.1 kHz sampling rate for optimal performance.
* Biquad filters are hard-coded and cannot be adjusted independently of each other.
* Unconventional voicing may not appeal to all users.

Best Use Cases:

* Adding weight and punch to bass guitar sounds.
* Enhancing heavy guitar sounds with a unique, aggressive tone.
* Experimental sound design and effects creation.

Tips on Optimal Settings:

* Adjust Low, Mid, and Treble controls to taste for optimal balance.
* Experiment with Drive levels to add desired amount of overdrive.
* Use Notch filter to shape high-frequency response.
