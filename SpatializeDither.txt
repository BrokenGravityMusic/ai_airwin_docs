SpatializeDither
A high-performance clarity and accuracy dither that excels at focus.

Key Controls and Settings:

* Type: Determines the type of dither to use (default: Contingent Dither).
* Blend Amount: Adjusts the balance between random noise and contingent dither.
* Quantization Value: Sets the quantization value for samples that hit perfectly on boundaries.


Best Use Cases:
Ideal for those seeking extreme clarity and accuracy in sound reproduction. Suitable for applications where precision is paramount, such as mastering or mixing critical tracks.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
Experiment with different Blend Amounts to find the desired balance between random noise and contingent dither. Adjust Quantization Value to suit specific applications.
