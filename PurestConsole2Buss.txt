Tips on Optimal Settings:
To achieve the best results, adjust Input Level to match the average signal level of your mix. Experiment with different Filter Types and Cutoff Frequencies to find the tone that suits your music or genre. Start with a low cutoff frequency (around 100 Hz) for a more subtle effect. Adjust resonance to taste, but be mindful of excessive resonance creating an unwanted "ringing" effect.
