Crystal

Tone shaper and buss soft-clipper that applies a unique EQ behavior and saturation effect to audio signals.

Key Controls and Settings:

* `Hardness:` Slider that defines the onset of clipping, controlling the "fatness" of the saturation effect (0-100)
	+ Range: 0% to 100%
	+ Type: Saturation control
* `Personality:` Parameter that adds dynamic behavior to the tone shaper (0-100)
	+ Range: 0% to 100%
	+ Type: Tonal coloration control

Caveats:
None specified

Best Use Cases:
Ideal for adding a unique EQ flavor and saturation effect to audio signals, particularly in buss processing. Suitable for applications where a specific tonal parameter is desired.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
Experiment with different Hardness settings to achieve desired levels of saturation and clipping onset. Adjust Personality to control the dynamic behavior of the tone shaper.
