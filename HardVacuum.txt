A tube style saturation effects plugin with a warmth control that brings in second harmonic and nonlinearity, as well as an Aura control that adds sparkly highs and hot searing overtones.

Key Controls and Settings:

* Warmth Control: adjusts the amount of second harmonic and nonlinearity.
    + Range: 
    + Type: 
* Aura Control: brings out sparkly highs and hot searing overtones.
    + Range: 0% to 100%
    + Type: 

Best Use Cases:

* Adding warmth and saturation to individual tracks or groups
* Enhancing high-frequency clarity and sparkle
