EveryTrim

A basic stereo trim plugin with Left/Right, Mid/Side, and Master level controls.

Key Controls and Settings:

* `Left`: Adjust the left channel level.
* `Right`: Adjust the right channel level.
* `Mid`: Adjust the mid channel level.
* `Side`: Adjust the side channel level.
* `Master`: Adjust the overall master level. Use the same noise shaping to the floating point buss as in PurestGain.

Tips on Optimal Settings:

Use EveryTrim for simple level adjustments, and consider using EdIsDim and MidSide for more complex processing between plugins.
