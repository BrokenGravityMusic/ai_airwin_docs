EveryTrim

A basic stereo trim plugin with Left/Right, Mid/Side, and Master level controls.

Key Controls and Settings:

* `Left`: Adjust the left channel level.
* `Right`: Adjust the right channel level.
* `Mid`: Adjust the mid channel level.
* `Side`: Adjust the side channel level.
* `Master`: Adjust the overall master level.
	+ Range: Use same noise shaping to floating point buss as in PurestGain
* Mix: A blend control for mixing the processed sound with the original (note: not explicitly listed in this plugin, but mentioned elsewhere)
	+ Range: 0% to 100%
