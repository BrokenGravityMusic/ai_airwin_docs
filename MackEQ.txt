MackEQ

A plugin simulating the Mackie distortion with added treble and bass controls.

Key Controls and Settings:

* Gain: Adjusts the overall gain level of the plugin.
	+ Range: 
	+ Type: 
* Treble: Controls the high-frequency response.
	+ Range: -24 dB to +12 dB
	+ Step size: 1 dB
* Bass: Controls the low-frequency response.
	+ Range: -24 dB to +12 dB
	+ Step size: 1 dB
* Distortion Level: Adjusts the amount of distortion applied to the signal.
	+ Range: 
	+ Type: 

Best Use Cases:

* Aggressively distorting sounds in electronic music production
* Creating a unique, overdriven sound for various musical styles

Tips on Optimal Settings:
* Start with moderate gain settings and adjust to taste.
* Adjust the treble and bass controls to taste, but be aware that excessive boosting may lead to distortion.
* Use the distortion level control to find the optimal balance between grit and noise floor.
