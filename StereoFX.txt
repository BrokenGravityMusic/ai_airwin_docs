StereoFX

A plugin with three functions that contour the stereo image:
- Stereo Wide: an aggressive widening effect
- Mono Bass: a highpass on the side channel
- Center Squish: a sine-based saturation or distortion on the mid channel only

Key Controls and Settings:

* `Stereo Wide`: 
  - Range: 0-100%
  - Adjusts the width of the stereo image with an aggressive effect
* `Mono Bass`: 
  - Frequency: Adjustable, range from low to high frequencies
  - Threshold: Adjustable, from -100 dB to 0 dB
  - A highpass filter on the side channel
* `Center Squish`: 
  - Range: 
  - Sine-based saturation or distortion on the mid channel only

Best Use Cases:
Ideal for adding an aggressive stereo widening effect to audio tracks, especially when combined with other plugins like Wider or High Impact.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
Experiment with different settings of Stereo Wide and Center Squish to achieve unique tone colorings. Combine Mono Bass with other plugins for added depth.
