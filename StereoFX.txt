StereoFX

An aggressive stereo widener.

Key Controls and Settings:

* `Stereo Wide`: Adjusts the width of the stereo image with an aggressive effect.
	+ Range: 0-100%
	+ Default Value:
* `Mono Bass`: A highpass filter on the side channel.
	+ Frequency: Adjustable, range from low to high frequencies
	+ Threshold: Adjustable, from -100 dB to 0 dB
* `Center Squish`: Sine-based saturation or distortion on the mid channel only.
	+ Range: 
	+ Default Value:

Caveats:
None noted.

Best Use Cases:
Ideal for music producers looking to add an aggressive widening effect to their stereo tracks.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
Experiment with different settings of Stereo Wide and Center Squish to achieve unique tone colorings. Combine Mono Bass with other plugins for added depth.
