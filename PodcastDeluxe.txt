PodcastDeluxe

A compressor with curve-style compression and phase rotators, designed to emulate radio station processing.

Key Controls and Settings:

* `Compressors:` 5 curve-like compressors in series
	+ Adjust the individual compressor settings to shape the overall compression response.
* Dry/Wet control: Allows adjustment of the compressed signal's blend with the original input

Best Use Cases:

* Mixing drums and percussion for high-impact dynamics processing
* Creating an interesting, processed sound on individual tracks
