Floor

A fake bottom octave plugin that simulates extended bass frequencies, generating an odd harmonic-related content to create the illusion of lower octaves.

Key Controls and Settings:
None mentioned in the provided text.

Caveats:
May not produce perfect results.
Can be considered "harmful" if used to deceive listeners about the actual frequency content of audio material.

Best Use Cases:
Use in situations where you want to simulate the presence of low frequencies, but may not have access to genuine bass content. Not recommended for mastering or critical listening applications.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
Consider using with caution and a clear understanding of its limitations to avoid unexpected results.
