YHighpass

A high-pass filter plugin that can produce varying textures from smooth to edgy, ideal for adding high-pass filtering effects to audio signals and enhancing clarity.

Key Controls and Settings:

* Type: The type of filtering (smooth to edgy)
    + Range: Not specified
    + Type: Not specified
* Frequency Cut-off: Adjusts the frequency at which filtering begins
    + Range: Not specified
    + Type: Not specified
* Resonance: Boosts or cuts specific frequencies for a more detailed sound
    + Range: Not specified
    + Type: Not specified

Best Use Cases:
Ideal for adding high-pass filtering effects to audio signals, such as enhancing clarity and reducing low-end noise.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
Adjusting the frequency cut-off and resonance controls can help achieve a balanced sound. Experiment with different settings to find the desired texture and tone.
