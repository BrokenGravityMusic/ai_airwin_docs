HighImpact


A distortion plugin that combines a distortion and anti-distortion circuit to reshape the transfer function of audio.


Key Controls and Settings:

* `Aggro:` A knob that controls the level of distortion and raspiness in the output sound.
	+ Range: Subtle to extreme
	+ Type: Knob
* Distorts quiet samples minimally, while amplifying medium samples significantly. 
	+ Range: 0% to 100%
	+ Type: 
* The plugin can be used on any type of audio, from percussive noises to instruments and vocals.

Best Use Cases:
Adding character and aggression to percussive noises or other audio elements.
