Thunder

A compressor plugin that retains or exaggerates subsonic bass when driven hard.

Key Controls and Settings:

* Threshold: Adjusts the level at which compression begins.
	+ Range: 
* Ratio: Sets the amount of compression applied to the signal.
	+ Range: 
* Attack: Controls how quickly the compressor responds to changes in the input signal.
	+ Range: 
* Release: Determines how long it takes for the compressor to return to its normal state after the input signal decreases.
	+ Range: 
* Mix: Blends the original and compressed signals.
	+ Range: A blend control for mixing the processed sound with the original
		- Range: 0% to 100%

Transform Factor: Adjusts the sensitivity and behavior of the compressor's subsonic bass modulation.

Caveats:
Requires careful monitoring of extreme lows, especially when pushing the plugin hard.
May not be suitable for all listening scenarios without proper subwoofer setup.

Best Use Cases:
Preparing music for large sound systems or high-end audiophile playback.
Extreme low-end enhancement for specific musical genres or effects.

Tips on Optimal Settings:
Start with gentle compression settings and gradually increase compression to achieve desired effect.
Pay attention to low-end response and adjust accordingly to avoid over-compression.
